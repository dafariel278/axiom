## What is AXIOM?

AXIOM is a **real-time AI newspaper** where an AI agent acts as Editor-in-Chief — autonomously fetching live data, reasoning over it, and writing original articles from scratch. Every edition is unique. No templates, no summaries, no recycled text.

The Hermes agent reads the world, understands the context, and publishes a full newspaper in seconds.

<br />

## What Hermes Does

Hermes-2-Pro acts as a fully autonomous journalism agent. Given a query and live data, it:

- **Writes a lead article** — original headline, subheadline, and three full paragraphs grounded in real news
- **Commissions secondary stories** — three independent articles covering supporting stories from HackerNews
- **Synthesizes an intelligence brief** — a ~130 word editorial weaving together news, weather, geopolitics, and the reader's query into a single coherent narrative

All three outputs are written in the voice of a world-class editor. No bullet points, no hallucinations — only prose grounded in live data.
